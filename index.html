Stay Healthy

Health Tips from Dr. Asati

Simple habits that make a big difference to your daily health.

💧

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Dehydration causes fatigue, headaches, and poor immunity.

🥗

Eat Balanced

Include fruits, vegetables, and protein in every meal. Avoid excessive oil, salt, and sugar.

🚶

Walk Daily

Just 30 minutes of walking each day reduces risk of diabetes, BP, and heart problems significantly.

😴

Sleep 7–8 Hours

Good sleep is the body's natural medicine. It repairs cells, boosts immunity, and reduces stress.
